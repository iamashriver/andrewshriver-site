Case Study: Design System + AI-Driven UX Transformation

Project: Designing at Scale: Systems Thinking and AI-Enabled UX
Project Duration: 2023-Present
Company: Janus Henderson Investors

Scroll Down
  • LinkedIn

Overview

As the UX function matured, the challenge shifted from building capability to scaling it. I led the evolution of our design system and introduced AI into our workflows to improve speed, quality, and insight generation.

At a Glance

Problem

  • Growing demand for digital experiences across regions and channels
  • Inefficiencies from one-off designs and inconsistent execution
  • Slow research synthesis and insight generation
  • Increasing complexity in maintaining design consistency

Approach

1. Design System at Scale
  • Built a component-driven design system in Figma with reusable assets and variables
  • Structured components at the global level, allowing updates to propagate across all projects
  • Enabled a drag-and-drop ecosystem for teams like Digital Delivery and Digital Change
2. Operational Efficiency
  • Introduced reusable templates and patterns to reduce bespoke work
  • Integrated UX into broader delivery workflows (Workfront, dev handoff improvements)
  • Leveraged external talent (Upwork) as scalable extensions of the team
3. AI Integration into UX
  • Introduced AI tools to:
    • Accelerate content creation and prototyping
    • Improve design iteration speed
    • Enhance research synthesis through AI-assisted analysis
  • Leveraged AI within user testing platforms to quickly identify patterns and insights
  • Positioned AI as a core capability, not an experiment

Impact

  • Significantly improved design speed and consistency
  • Reduced reliance on one-off design solutions
  • Accelerated research-to-insight timelines
  • Increased team productivity and scalability
  • Established a foundation for AI-enabled UX workflows

Leadership

I led this transformation by combining design systems thinking with emerging AI capabilities, ensuring the team not only adopted new tools but fundamentally changed how we design and deliver experiences.
